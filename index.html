Minar-e-Pakistan

Minar-e-Pakistan (Tower of Pakistan) is a landmark national monument in Lahore, Punjab, located in Greater Iqbal Park. It stands at the exact site where the Lahore Resolution (later called the Pakistan Resolution) was passed on March 23, 1940, which was the first official call for a separate homeland for Muslims in British India.
